The world of artificial intelligence has grown exponentially over the past decade, weaving its way into nearly every aspect of daily life. From virtual assistants that help manage our schedules to advanced algorithms that predict stock market trends, AI is revolutionizing how we interact with technology and the world around us. This rapid advancement comes with its own set of challenges, including ethical considerations, privacy concerns, and the potential displacement of jobs.
